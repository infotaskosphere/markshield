🔑 What is a TMA Code?
Your unique identifier issued by CGPDTM to registered trademark attorneys. This is typically a numeric code (e.g. 25092) or in the format TMA/XX/NNNN (e.g. TMA/GJ/2847). Enter exactly as shown in your CGPDTM registration certificate.
